What is Catagories in Wordpress ?

Categories are one of the two predefined taxonomies in WordPress (the other being tags). They’re a way of grouping related content; think of them as being like labels.

Categories are the most general method of grouping content on a WordPress site. A category symbolizes a topic or a group of topics that are connected to one another in some way.

To edit the categories on your site, go to Posts / Categories:

For instance, if I write a post announcing a piece of latest news, I’ll put it in the “News” category. If no category is added, then by default category is uncategorized .

Edit Post

There are two options to edit. One is Edit and other one is Quick Edit. On clicking Edit, following page will appear.

The other option is Quick Edit , which allow us to edit title, date and slug of your post. And if want, you can also change category for your post.

Delete Post

On All posts option you can choose Trash/bin option which will let you delete the respective post.
